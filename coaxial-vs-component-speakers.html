Coaxial vs Component Speakers: What’s the Difference?

Coaxials combine woofer+tweeter in one unit — simple install. Components separate them for better imaging but require more install effort.

  • Choose coaxials for a fast drop-in replacement and budget builds.
  • Choose components for soundstage and detail (with proper mounting and tuning).
