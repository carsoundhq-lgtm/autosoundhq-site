How to Tune a Car Amp (Beginner's Guide)

  1. Start with gains at minimum; set HU volume to ~75%.
  2. Enable high-pass/low-pass crossovers appropriately (e.g., 80Hz HPF for door speakers).
  3. Increase gain until loud but clean; back off if distortion appears.
